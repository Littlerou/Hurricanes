Hurricane Debby

Hurricane Debby lasted from 31st August to 5th September, and was a category 1 hurricane.

Hurricane Florence

Hurricane Florence lasted from 7th September to 11th September, and was a category 1 hurricane.

Hurricane Gilbert

Hurricane Gilbert lasted from 8th September to 19th September, and was a category 5 hurricane.

Hurricane Helene

Hurricane Helene lasted from 19th September to 30th September, and was a category 4 hurricane.

Hurricane Joan

Hurricane Joan lasted from 10th October to 23rd October, and was a category 4 hurricane.
